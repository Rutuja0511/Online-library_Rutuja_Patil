  • Under Graduate Bachelor of Electronics Engineering Intake: 120

4. ELECTRONICS AND TELECOMMUNICATION ENGINEERING

  • Under Graduate Bachelor of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering Intake:120
  • Post Graduate Master of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering Intake:18

5. BIOMEDICAL ENGINEERING
