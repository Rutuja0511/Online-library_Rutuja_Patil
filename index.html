sci-fi

Broad genre of fiction that often involves speculations based on current or future science or technology. Science fiction is found in books, art, television, films, games, theatre, and other media. In organizational or marketing contexts, science fiction can be synonymous with the broader definition of speculative fiction, encompassing creative works incorporating imaginative elements not found in contemporary reality; this includes fantasy, horror and related genres.

Comtemporary Books

Contemporary fiction books are made up of stories that could happen to real people in real situations. Other categories or genres do not apply to the books. They usually take place in the same time period as the reader (the present), with events that are currently occurring or could occur in the same time period.
